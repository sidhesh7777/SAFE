Government Welfare Schemes

This section provides details about government schemes available for sanitation workers and their families.

Health Protection Scheme

Sanitation workers are provided free medical checkups and healthcare support through government hospitals. Annual health screening programs are conducted to ensure worker safety.

Financial Support Scheme

Workers are eligible for financial assistance, emergency relief funds, and pension benefits. The government provides subsidies and welfare payments for sanitation workers.

Education Support

Children of sanitation workers can receive educational scholarships and financial support for school and college education through special welfare programs.

Insurance Coverage

Sanitation workers are covered under accident insurance and life insurance schemes provided by municipal welfare departments.
